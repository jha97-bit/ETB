What is the profit we will have?
- Revenue Per Card: $79 Annual Fee + $15 x 12 Annual Interest = $259
- Cost Per Card: $5 x 12 Annual Fraud Cost = $60.
- Profit = Revenue - Cost = ($259 - $60) x 500,000 customers = 99.5M
